Frequently Asked Questions
1. What is your payment policy for renting a bike?

Eazyrental only accepts online payments. We charge hourly rates based on weekdays, weekends, and peak seasons.

2. Is booking cancellation allowed?

Yes, bookings can be canceled from the booking page. Please check our cancellation policy.

3. What is your booking cancellation policy?

- Cancellation more than 24 hours before booking: ₹100 charge.
- Cancellation within 24 hours: ₹100 or 50% of the booking fee (whichever is greater).
- Cancellation after booking start: No refund.

4. Can I change my bike after booking?

Yes, you can change the bike using the "Exchange Vehicle" option before the booking starts.

5. What happens if I return the bike late?

A late return charge of ₹200 per hour will be applied in addition to the full hourly rental rate.

6. Is fuel included with the rental?

No, fuel is not included. You will receive enough fuel to reach the nearest petrol pump.

7. What is the minimum age to rent a bike?

The minimum age requirement is 21 years.

8. Do you provide a helmet?

Yes, we provide one complimentary helmet. Additional helmets cost ₹50 each.

9. Can I take the rented bike across international borders?

No, our bikes are not allowed outside India.

10. What happens in case of an accident?

The customer is liable for 100% of damage costs and any business loss due to downtime.
